AMRITA VISHWA VIDYAPEETHAM

AMRITA VISHWA VIDYAPEETHAM is a renowned institution dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation. Our campus is a vibrant mix of traditional values and modern facilities that support academic and personal growth.

Library & Knowledge Center

Our central library houses over 50,000 books, national and international journals, and a digital archive — a peaceful haven for research and study.

Laboratories & Innovation Labs

From AI to Embedded Systems, our advanced labs offer hands-on learning with real-world tools and technologies.

Auditorium & Seminar Halls

Modern AV-equipped auditoriums host inspiring seminars, fests, and cultural showcases every semester.

Sports & Fitness

We offer sports facilities, gymnasiums, and grounds that support both recreation and competition across various disciplines.

Hostels & Cafeteria

Our hostels are modern and comfortable. Cafeteria provides hygienic and delicious vegetarian options.

Clubs & Student Life

Clubs in tech, music, photography, and literature ensure a rich and fulfilling campus experience for every student.
